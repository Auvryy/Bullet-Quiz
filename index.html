How to play?

Bullet Quiz is a fast-paced trivia game where you answer as many questions as possible within 1 minute. Your goal is to score as high as you can by answering correctly and quickly.

  1. Choose Your Category

    Select a category from the dropdown menu. You can pick a specific topic like General Knowledge, Science, Sports, etc. If you want a mix of all categories, leave this option blank.

  2. Select Difficulty Level

    Select a category from the dropdown menu. You can pick a specific topic like General Knowledge, Science, Sports, etc. If you want a mix of all categories, leave this option blank.

  3. Start The Quiz

    Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin. A question will appear along with four multiple-choice answers.

  4. Answer Quickly

    You have a total of 60 seconds to answer as many questions as you can. After you select an answer, a new question will load immediately. There is no time penalty for wrong answers, so focus on speed.

  5. Score Points

    Each correct answer will give you 1 point. The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible before time runs out.

  6. Game Over

    Once the 60-second timer reaches zero, the game will end and your final score will be displayed. You can restart the quiz to try and beat your high score.

    Tips:

  • Answer quickly without overthinking; speed is key.
  • Difficult questions are worth the same points as easy ones, so it's about how fast you can answer.
  • Replaying multiple rounds helps improve your familiarity with the questions and categories.
